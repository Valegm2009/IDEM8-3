Enfermedades Y sintomas en Ingles

1. AN ALLERGY: A negative reaction to something you eat or touch UNA ALERGIA: una reacción negativa a algo que comes o tocas You should apply a cream to calm the allergy
2 ASTHMA: Respiratory problem. Difficulty of breathing ASMA: Problema respiratorio. Dificultad para respirar You should use a breather to be able to breathe well
3. A BACKACHE: A prolonged pain in the back. UN DOLOR DE ESPALDA: Un dolor prolongado en la espalda. You should It consult a doctor
4. A BROKEN LEG: A bone in the leg is broken. UNA PIERNA ROTA: Se rompe un hueso de la pierna. You should to consult a specialist
5. A COUGH: The act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound. TOS: El acto de expulsar el aire de los pulmones con un sonido agudo repentino. You should take a syrup to calm the cough
6. A COLD: A common viral infection which causes mucus to run from the nose, gives a sore throat and often includes UN RESFRIADO: Una infección viral común que hace que la mucosidad salga de la nariz, produce dolor de garganta y a menudo incluye You should drink something hot to soothe the cold a bit.
7. AN EARACHE: Pain inside the ear. DOLOR DE OÍDO: Dolor dentro del oído. You should go to the otolaryngologist to check your ears.
8. A FEVER: An abnormally high body temperature, usually accompanied by shivering and a headache. FIEBRE: Una temperatura corporal anormalmente alta, generalmente acompañada de escalofríos y dolor de cabeza. You should take lukewarm showers
9. THE FLU: Is the common name given for influenza. It is a contagious viral infection of the respiratory passages that causes fever and sever aching. LA GRIPE: Es el nombre común que se le da a la influenza. Es una infección viral contagiosa de las vías respiratorias que causa fiebre y dolor severo. You should to rest and take the medicine prescribed by the doctor
10. A HEADACHE: A continuous pain in the head. UN DOLOR DE CABEZA: Un dolor continuo en la cabeza. You should to take some type of analgesic initially, if the pain does not stop, visit the doctor
11. HEARTBURN: A form of indigestion felt as a burning sensation in the chest. Acidez estomacal: Una forma de indigestión que se siente como una sensación de ardor en el pecho. You should avoid spicy and fatty foods.
12. A RASH: A lot of small red spots on the skin that are usually itchy.. UNA ERUPCIÓN: Una gran cantidad de pequeñas manchas rojas en la piel que generalmente pican. You should avoid scratching or rubbing the rash to prevent further irritation.
13. A SORE THROAT: A condition of pain in the throat, typically caused by inflammation of it. DOLOR DE GARGANTA: Una condición de dolor en la garganta, generalmente causada por la inflamación de la misma. You should rest your voice and avoid speaking loudly or shouting.
14. A SPRAIN: An injury to a joint in your body, especially your wrist or ankle, caused by suddenly twisting it. UN ESGUINCE: Una lesión en una articulación de su cuerpo, especialmente en la muñeca o el tobillo, causada por una torcedura repentina. You should rest and avoid putting weight on the injured area.
15. A STOMACHACHE (US) STOMACH ACHE (Brit): The pain in a person's belly. DOLOR DE ESTÓMAGO (EE. UU.) DOLOR DE ESTÓMAGO (británico): El dolor en el vientre de una persona. You should avoid eating heavy or spicy meals.
16. A TOOTHACHE: The pain in a tooth or teeth. UN DOLOR DE MUELAS: El dolor en un diente o dientes. You should rinse your mouth with warm saltwater.
